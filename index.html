About Jiu-Jitsu Dummy

Jiu jitsu is a martial art that focuses on close-contact grappling and ground fighting techniques. The goal of jiu jitsu is to get your opponent to submit… whether by choke, joint-break, or sheer… freakin… dominance.

This dummy's purpose is mostly to teach people that do not practice jiu-jitsu what submissions can be applied to certain body parts. Simply click on a body part, and a list of submissions that you can perform will appear.

Keep in mind, if we were to compare jiu-jitsu to soccer (a sport EVERYONE knows), submissions would be similar to scoring a goal. Both in jiu-jitsu and soccer, finishing may be what everyone wants to see, but it is a really minor part of the sport. Most of the time in both sports is spent building up to get to the point where you can actually finish.
